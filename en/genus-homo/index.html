Genus Homo Publishing

Genus Homo is an independent publishing imprint founded by Olmo Gómez Aldaz.

Its purpose is to publish the author’s own works and, eventually, those of other authors within an autonomous editorial framework.

The name refers to the biological genus Homo, to which the human species belongs. It does not point to a closed thematic focus, but to a broad framework connected to the human condition in its historical, cultural and reflective dimensions.

Publisher Information

Location: Tudela de Duero (Valladolid), Spain.

Year of establishment: 2025.

Responsible: Olmo Gómez Aldaz.

Publications

Titles published under this imprint are available on Amazon and may be ordered through bookstores via international distribution.

Books published from the process of identity restitution

DOGA 2025. The Battle for My Identity

English edition

Author: Olmo Gómez Aldaz.
Publisher: Genus Homo.
Published in February 2026.

First-person account of an identity theft and fraudulent adoption that took place in Bilbao, Spain, in 1971. The book originates as a formal declaration submitted under the Democratic Memory framework and was later developed into a public text combining personal testimony, documentary evidence, and legal proceedings. It reconstructs the author’s life history, the concealment of his origins, and the path leading to the judicial restitution of biological filiation, including the final court rulings and their effects.

Available from Amazon stores and local bookstores.
